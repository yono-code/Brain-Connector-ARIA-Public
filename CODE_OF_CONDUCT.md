# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project expects contributors to communicate and collaborate respectfully.
Harassment, discrimination, doxxing, intimidation, and abusive behavior are not allowed.

## Expected Behavior

- Use clear and respectful language.
- Focus feedback on code, design, and outcomes.
- Assume good intent and discuss disagreements constructively.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks or insults
- Sexualized language or unwanted attention
- Publishing private information without consent
- Threats or sustained disruptive behavior

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block contributors for violations.

To report conduct issues, contact:

- `yonogames.dev@gmail.com`

Reports will be reviewed confidentially to the extent possible.
